## 2. Background and Reason for the Project

The project began as a plan for **FonAI Labs**, a parent innovation group, startup factory, and company-building ecosystem that would hold multiple projects, products, and companies.

After a survey, people were pronouncing or reading **FonAI** as **“Fon AI”**, which made the brand look too much like an AI-only company. Since the purpose of the parent company is much broader than artificial intelligence, the name was changed.

The final agreed name is:

# Fonia Labs

This change solves the AI-only confusion and makes the brand feel more flexible, natural, global, and capable of housing software and non-software companies in the future.
